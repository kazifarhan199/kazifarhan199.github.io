FreeMind


FreeMind is a mobile application designed to address the mental health challenges faced by college students, especially in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. Recognizing the heightened stress and emotional health decline among students, FreeMind aims to provide a supportive platform for social interaction and personalized well-being recommendations. You can find the source code at github.com/kazifarhan199/FreeMind-APP
